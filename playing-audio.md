# 🎚️ Playing Audio

You can play audio files from:
✅ **Project View** – Click **Play** to listen to tracks.  
✅ **Feedback View** – Play mixes or stems and interact with comments.

### 🎛 Playback Controls:
🔁 Restart Playback  
⏪ Rewind / ⏩ Skip Ahead 15s  
⏯️ Play / Pause  
🛑 Stop
